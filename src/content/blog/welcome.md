in `src/content/blog/`. The filename (minus extension) becomes the URL slug.

## Writing posts

Frontmatter fields are validated against the schema in
`src/content.config.ts`. The required fields are `title`, `description`, and
`pubDate`. Optional fields include `updatedDate`, `tags`, and `draft`.

- Set `draft: true` to keep a post out of the build.
- Use standard Markdown, or rename the file to `.mdx` to embed components.
